Smart Campus Initiatives

These initiatives aim to provide benefits for the University Campus by using smart technologies which support discovering operating efficiencies, saving money, improving the student experience, helping optimize teaching and learning, improving campus safety, etc. Smart technologies can reduce wait times, improve student services, automate workflows, and conserve energy and other resources.
