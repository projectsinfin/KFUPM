About KFUPM

King Fahd University of Petroleum & Minerals (KFUPM) is one of the leading educational organizations. It aims at being a preeminent institution known for its globally competitive graduates, cutting edge research, and leadership in energy discoveries.

KFUPM is committed to make a substantial impact in what it does. Today, KFUPM offers 83 programs to more than 10,000 students studying in its. 8 Academic Colleges

KFUPM Fund

The aim of the KFUPM fund is to provide an impact driven investment opportunity through a leading educational & research funding in the Kingdom. With its large alumni network and network of charitable institutions the fund preserves the true essence of truth and provides a sustainable distribution for our Contributors.
