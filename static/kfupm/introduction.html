Introduction

KFUPM

King Fahd University of Petroleum & Minerals (KFUPM) is one of the leading educational organizations in the region. It aims at being a preeminent institution known for its globally competitive graduates, cutting edge research & leadership in energy discoveries.

KFUPM is committed to making a difference in all what it does. Today, KFUPM offers 83 programs to more than 10,000 students studying in its 8 Academic Colleges

KFUPM Fund

King Fahd University of Petroleum and Minerals (KFUPM) is a world-renowned center of education and research. The University has pursued a quest for excellence to ensure that through quality research & education, the leaders of tomorrow are prepared, today.

Throughout its six decades. KFUPM has always sought to be at the leading edge of research across all its faculties.

However, the cost of providing such facilities tends to increase annually and the KFUPM Fund allows us to bridge the gap in order to allow the university to cater to the next generation of leaders.

Although the University is funded by the Saudi government, the Fund Office (established. 2006) provides additional funds directly to research, education and student enrichment activities that cannot usually be funded by the regular operating budget.
